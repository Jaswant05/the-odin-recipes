Biriyani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or lamb,cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions,thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 2 green chilly
  • 1/2cup of chooped mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoon biriyani masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • salt to taste
  • saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice throughly and soak it in water for about 30 minutes.Drain and set aside
  2. Marinate the chicken or lamb with yogrut,half of the sliced onions,half nof the chopped tomatoes,garlic,ginger,green chilles,half of the cilantro,half of the mint leavs,biriyani masala,tumeric powder,let it Marinatefor 30 minutes
  3. In large pot,heat ghee or oilover medium heat.Add the remaning sliced onions and saute until gloden brown.Remove half of the onions ans set them aside for garnish
  4. Add the marinated chicken to the post and cook untill it's halfway done
  5. In a separate pot,bring water to boil,add salt and soaked rice.Cook the rice until it's 70% done then drain it
  6. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken in the pot.sprinkle the remaningchopped tomatoes,cilantro and mint leaves on top
  7. cover the pot with tight fitting lid and cook on low flame for about 20-25 minutes,or until the rice and meat are fully cooked
  8. once done,gently fluff up the biriyani with fork.Garnish with reserved fried onions and saffron-infused milk
  9. Serve hot with rita
